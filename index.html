About Me

Hello! My name is Lillian Kennedy, and I am currently studying Applied Business and Management while also learning web development. I am developing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to better understand how modern websites are designed, built, and maintained. Through this course, I am working on different projects that help me practice creating responsive, accessible, and user-friendly web pages. I enjoy learning how technology and design work together to create useful digital experiences for people. As I continue studying web development, I hope to improve my problem-solving skills and gain more experience building websites from the ground up. I am especially interested in learning how websites can be both functional and visually appealing while providing a positive experience for users. This course gives me the opportunity to explore these ideas and apply what I learn through hands-on assignments and projects. This website will serve as a place to share my coursework, projects, and progress as I continue developing my knowledge and skills in web development.
